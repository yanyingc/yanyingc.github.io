Recent Proejects

Medical Image Report Generation, 2018 - present, at FXPAL

Adapted context-aware image captioning to medical imaging domains. Proposed the model that addressed the data bias problem in medical image reports and the need of higher accuracy on the descriptions of abnormalities. Lead the research and communication with collaborators.

Customer/Location Profiling and Image/Video Captioning, 2015 - 2017, at FXPAL

Leveraged deep learning technologies for identifying business venues and customer purchase stage from social media images and posts, and generating image captions relevant to business activities therein. Designed models that integrate context cross domains/tasks to improve and enrich image/video captioning.

Viewer Affective Comment Generation, 2013 - 2014, at Columbia University

Leveraged NLP and Affective Computing to discover the relations between image content and the evokedviewer affects in its comments and generate affective comments for social media images.
